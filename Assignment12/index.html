Music Discord Bot

Much of my free time is spent tinkering with this bot. The problem that I see with many other bots is their clunky, non-intuitive UI and ambiguous commands. My bot aims to fix that problem by removing commands entirely while providing users with the quality listening experience to which they are accustomed.

Wall Music has its own text channel. Instead of having to type /play, you can type directly into the text channel to have your song displayed. Instead of clunky commands, update your bot's settings directly in the app.

Most bots do not give user's the option to reorder the queue, so Wall Music has it's own React based Web App! Users can search for songs using the integrated YouTube Data v3 API, log in using Discord's Auth0 feature, and play music in their server with more control than any other bot.
